Welcome to the Assignment App

This is a simple assignment management app.

The Assignment App is a streamlined academic management tool that helps students and educators organize, track, and complete coursework efficiently. It allows users to create and manage assignments with deadlines, receive timely reminders, categorize tasks by subject or priority, and collaborate seamlessly between teachers and students for submission and feedback. With built‑in progress tracking and performance insights, the app reduces missed deadlines, improves productivity, and enhances the overall learning workflow in a simple, user‑friendly interface.
